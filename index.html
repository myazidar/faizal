About Me

Faizal Razak

I'm a graphic designer with over 12 years of experience crafting visual identities that resonate. Based in San Francisco, I've had the privilege of working with startups, Fortune 500 companies, and everything in between.

My design philosophy centers on the belief that great design is invisible — it should feel so natural that it becomes an extension of the brand itself. I combine strategic thinking with aesthetic precision to create work that not only looks beautiful but performs.
